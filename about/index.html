About Me

Photographer & Cinematographer | San Antonio, TX

Dominic Martinez

I'm Dominic Martinez, a passionate photographer and cinematographer based in San Antonio, TX. With a background in Digital Cinematography from Full Sail University and extensive experience in photography and video production, I bring stories to life through my lens.

My work captures emotions and moments, blending artistic vision with technical precision to create unforgettable visuals. Whether it's a portrait session, a film project, or an architectural photo, I approach every project with dedication and creativity.

Beyond the technical aspects of photography and cinematography, I'm passionate about visual storytelling. Each project is an opportunity to connect with people and preserve memories that will last a lifetime. I believe in the power of images to communicate emotions and tell compelling stories.
